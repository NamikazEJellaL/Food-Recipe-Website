Chicken Parmesan

Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
Salt and pepper to taste
1 cup (125g) breadcrumbs
1/2 cup (50g) grated Parmesan cheese
2 eggs, beaten
1 cup (240ml) marinara sauce
1 cup (120g) shredded mozzarella cheese
Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Grease a baking dish.
Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
In one bowl, mix breadcrumbs and grated Parmesan. In another bowl, beat the eggs.
Dip each chicken breast into the beaten eggs, then into the breadcrumb and cheese mixture, coating evenly.
Place the coated chicken breasts in the prepared baking dish and bake for 20-25 minutes or until the chicken is fully cooked.
Spoon marinara sauce over each chicken breast and top with shredded mozzarella cheese.
Return to the oven and bake until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
Garnish with fresh basil leaves before serving.


Enjoy this savory and comforting Chicken Parmesan dish!
